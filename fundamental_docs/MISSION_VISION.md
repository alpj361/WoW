# WoW Mission & Vision

## Mission
**"To empower social connection and unite people through local cultural events, moving beyond standard mall gatherings to offer meaningful, diverse real-world experiences."**

*(Spanish: "Potenciar la conexión social y unir a las personas a través de eventos culturales locales, yendo más allá de las típicas reuniones en centros comerciales para ofrecer experiencias reales y significativas.")*

## Vision
**"To become the heartbeat of social life, where every plan is effortless, every interaction is memorable, and every user feels the 'WoW' factor—guided by the spirit of Mr. Pez to take fun seriously."**

*(Spanish: "Convertirse en el latido de la vida social, donde cada plan es sencillo, cada interacción memorable y cada usuario siente el factor 'WoW'—guiados por el espíritu de Mr. Pez para tomar la diversión en serio.")*

## Core Values
1.  **Culture Over Commerce**: We prioritize authorized, unique local events over generic gatherings.
2.  **Serious Fun**: We build robust, reliable tools (Attendance, Payments) wrapped in an engaging, vibrant experience.
3.  **Seamless Connection**: Removing friction from "Let's hang out" to "See you there".

---

## Features & Style Overview

### Key Features
1.  **Event Discovery & Creation**:
    -   **Create Events**: Via Manual Entry, Flyer Upload (Camera/Gallery), **Instagram URL** (with AI analysis), and **WhatsApp** (Coming Soon).
    -   Background extraction of Instagram content.
    -   Draft system for saving event creations in progress.
2.  **Social Interactions**:
    -   **Event Reactions**: Thread-style reactions with emojis and comments for attended events.
    -   **Digital Profile Card**: Interactive 3D card with QR code for easy sharing (`wow://user/{id}`).
3.  **Host Capabilities**:
    -   **"Anfitrión" Mode**: Dashboard for hosting events.
    -   **Attendance Tracking**: QR Scanner for hosts to validate guests.
    -   Guest list management with payment receipt viewing.
4.  **Integrations**:
    -   **WhatsApp**: Planned/In Progress for sending flyers.
    -   Calendar and Map integrations for event details.

### Visual Style
-   **Theme**: Dark Mode primarily (`#0F0F0F` background).
-   **Aesthetics**: **Glassmorphism** (frosted glass effects, blur), **Neon/Glow** effects.
-   **Color Palette**:
    -   **Primary Gradient**: Purple (`#8B5CF6`) → Pink (`#D946EF`) → Red (`#F43F5E`).
    -   **Accents**: Neon glow, White text with high contrast.
    -   **Mascot**: **Mr. Pez** (Official Mascot).
-   **Vibe**: "Juguetón pero serio" (Playful yet serious).
